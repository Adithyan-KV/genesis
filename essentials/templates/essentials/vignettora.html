Guidelines
  • One entry per participant will be allowed. Please note that this must strictly be an original work by the participant.
  • The story must be strictly below 1500 words, adhering to the prompt given.t to participants.
  • The competition proceeds in two rounds, the prelims, and the final round. Topics and deadlines for each of the rounds will be announced shortly (CHECK FOR UPDATES ON THE WEBSITE!)
  • The entries may be submitted online through the submission portal, following registrations.
  • Late submissions will NOT be considered at ANY cost.
  • The decision of the panel of judges is binding in terms of choice of the winners.
