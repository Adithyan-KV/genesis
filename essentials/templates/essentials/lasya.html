Lasya

With an exquisite blend of rhythm and poise, a touch of innovation, and a footfall of more than a thousand, Lasya represents the dance exponent of Pravega, and displays the delicate balance of passion and grace in our life.

It comprises of 3 events – Solo Dance, Duet Dance and Group Dance.

The group event will have teams comprising of a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 25 members.

Preliminaries
Video Preliminaries:

Video must be uploaded to the website portal or YouTube/Vimeo link must be provided at time of registration. The camera must remain stationary and the recording must be a single take.

The duration of video must not exceed 3 minutes.

The results of prelims will be declared latest by 1st November. 6 teams will be selected from the entries pool for Lasya finals in each event, i.e. a total of 18 teams.

General Rules of the Event:
  • Teams must bring their own props and costumes
  • Maximum duration allowed for the performance for FINALS is 10 minutes (including set-up time).
  • Teams exceeding the stipulated time limit will be penalized and those crossing 3 minutes extra-time will be disqualified.
  • Use of fire (even diyas, candles or lighters) and water is not allowed.
Registration

Registrations will be accepted online through the website prior to the event. No spot registrations are allowed. A refundable security deposit of ₹1000 will be charged at time of registration.

Registrations close on 15th October.
