Battle of Bands

Battle of Bands is the annual band competition of Pravega.

The event will comprise of two primary levels followed by the finals which will be held at IISc during Pravega:

Preliminaries
Online Preliminaries:

Audio/Video must be uploaded to the website portal or YouTube/Vimeo/SoundCloud link must be provided at time of registration. The camera must remain stationary and the recording must be a single take.

The duration of audio/video must not exceed 3 minutes. Type of file : {{ file_info }}.

Preliminaries
2nd Round

The 2nd round of preliminaries will be held live in the following cities- Bangalore, Chennai, Kochi, Mumbai, Hyderabad.

Preliminaries
2nd Round

6 teams selected from each region from the 1st Prelims pool will participate in the 2nd Prelims which will be held at pubs, jam pads and studios in the cities mentioned above tentatively in the months of October and November.

The winners from each region will be given exciting cash prizes and will get to participate in the Pravega finals. The finale winners receive a generous cash prize, amount TBA.

General Rules of the Event:
  • In the preliminaries, the performances (including set-up time) should not exceed a duration of 30 minutes.
  • In the finals, the maximum duration of performance can be 45 minutes (inclusive of a 5 minutes set-up time).
  • Teams exceeding the stipulated time limit will be penalized and those crossing 10 minutes extra time will be disqualified.
Registration

Registrations will be accepted online through the website prior to the event. No spot registrations are allowed.
