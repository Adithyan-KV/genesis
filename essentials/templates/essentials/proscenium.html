Proscenium is the Thespian Extravaganza of Pravega, in which some of our countryâ€™s most talented student theatre groups argue for a debatable statement presented a-priori. The plays can be in English, Hindi, or Kannada.

Preliminaries
Video Preliminaries:

The preliminaries will be held during September and October. The camera must remain stationary and the recording must be a single take. The duration of the video must not exceed 10 minutes.

The results of the preliminaries will be declared latest by 1st November. 6-8 teams will be selected from the entries pool for the final event. Plays with social relevance are expected for the prelims.

General Rules of the Event:
  • Each team must comprise of a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 20 individuals (exclusive of all the helpers).
  • The teams must bring their own props and costumes.
  • The theme for the finals will be announced with the results of the prelims.
  • The maximum duration of the play is 30 minutes (this includes 5 minutes set-up time). Teams exceeding the stipulated time limit will be penalized and those crossing 5 minutes extra-time will be disqualified.
  • Use of fire (even diyas, candles or lighters) and water is not allowed.
Registration

Registrations will be accepted online through the website prior to the event. No spot registrations will be allowed.
